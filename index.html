Here! we are going to help you know which day of the week you were born

But first we are giving you a guide on how to do it.

What you enter in the form

  1. Your day of birth
  2. Your month of birth
  3. Your century of birth. For example if you were born in 1989. Your century is 19
  4. Your year of birth. For example if you were born in 1989. Your year is 89

Then you are going to press the submit button, and just like that you are going to know the day of the week you were born
